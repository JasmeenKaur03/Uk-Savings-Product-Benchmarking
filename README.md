# UK Digital Bank Savings Product Benchmarking | Pinnacle Savings
**Tool:** Microsoft Excel | **Duration:** October 2025 | **Domain:** Retail Banking / Deposit Products / Business Intelligence
 
---
 
## Overview
 
This project is a competitive benchmarking analysis of retail savings and deposit products offered by six UK digital banks as of October 2025. It identifies market positioning, rate competitiveness, and product gaps — and produces an investor-facing recommendation matrix matching saver profiles to the best available product.
 
---
 
## Business Problem
 
Retail savers and investors choosing between UK digital banks face a complex product landscape with varying rates, deposit limits, terms, and access methods. This analysis was designed to provide structured, data-driven guidance by comparing the full product range across leading digital banks and identifying which institutions offer the strongest value for different investor profiles.

## What the File Contains
 
**Sheet 1 — Product Comparison**
A comparison of 14 savings products across all six banks, including product type (Easy Access, Fixed Term, Notice Account), AER (%), minimum and maximum deposit limits (GBP), notice/term period, FSCS protection status, access method, and a live Rate Rank formula ranking each product by AER within the dataset.
 
A colour scale on the AER column provides instant visual identification of the highest and lowest rates across the market.
 
**Sheet 2 — Competitive Analysis**
Two sections:
- Bank-level summary comparing each institution's best easy access rate, best fixed term rate, maximum deposit limit, FSCS status, and market positioning
- Competitive advantage analysis for Pinnacle Savings, rating each differentiator as Strong / Neutral / Parity / Differentiator with strategic implications
 
**Sheet 3 — Recommendation Matrix**
Maps seven investor profiles (Conservative Saver, Moderate Saver, Growth-Focused Saver, Young Saver, High Net Worth Individual, Business Owner/Entrepreneur, Rate-Focused Saver) to the most suitable bank and product, with AER and rationale. Pinnacle Savings is recommended for five of seven profiles based on rate and limit competitiveness.
 
---
 
## Key Formulas Used
 
- RANK — live rate ranking across all 14 products
- ColorScaleRule — three-colour gradient on AER column (red to yellow to green)
- Conditional Formatting — strength ratings colour-coded across competitive advantage table
 
---
 
## Key Findings
 
- Pinnacle Savings offers the highest Easy Access AER (4.72%) and the highest 1-Year Fixed Rate (5.02%) in the comparison set
- Pinnacle's maximum deposit limit of GBP 500,000 is five times higher than Apex and Bloom (GBP 100,000)
- Sterling Digital offers the highest deposit cap (GBP 1,000,000) but the lowest Easy Access AER (3.25%)
- Apex Digital Bank is the strongest option for younger savers due to its GBP 50 minimum deposit threshold
- All six banks offer full FSCS protection — this is market standard and not a differentiator
